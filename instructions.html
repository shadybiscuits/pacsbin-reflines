One-Time Setup (30 seconds)

Step 1: Make sure your browser's bookmarks bar is visible.
Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac)
Step 2: Drag the blue button below to your bookmarks bar:

🔗 PacsBin Ref Lines
Drag the blue button to your bookmarks bar, or copy and paste as a new bookmark URL.

That's it for setup. The bookmarklet stays in your bookmarks bar permanently.

How to Use (Every Case)

1. Open a PacsBin case link as normal.
2. Click the grid icon in the toolbar and select a multi-panel layout (e.g. 1x2 for side-by-side). Make sure each panel shows a different series.
3. Click "PacsBin Ref Lines" in your bookmarks bar.
4. You will see a confirmation alert. Cross-plane series (e.g. axial + sagittal): green dashed reference lines appear. Same-plane series (e.g. 3 axials side-by-side): scrolling one viewport syncs the others to the matching slice position.
Note: Click the bookmarklet once per case (it resets on page navigation). If you change the layout after activating, click it again.

Troubleshooting

  • "Open a PacsBin case first" - Navigate to a PacsBin case before clicking the bookmarklet.
  • No lines visible - You need a multi-panel layout with different series in each panel.
  • Lines appear but don't move - Click the bookmarklet again to re-attach handlers.
